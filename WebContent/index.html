About this system


12 Engineering Graduate Attributes

As prescribed by the NBA
GA 1 Engineering Knowledge
GA 2 Problem Analysis
GA 3 Development of Solution
GA 4 Investigations of Inauspicious Problems
GA 5 Modern Tool Usage
GA 6 Impact on Society and Environment
GA 7 Environment and Stability
GA 8 Ethics
GA 9 Communication
GA 10 Individual and Team Work
GA 11 Project Management and Finance
GA 12 Life-Long Learning






Graduate attributes are of immense importance for the students, as well as their respective institute, and they need to be achieved during a students’ full course of academics to ensure their development as an ideal Engineering Graduate. Analysing a student’s performance during their academics is crucial for increasing the chances of employability.

This system provides a platform wherein a student can track, manage, analyse and visualize their performance based on the graduate attributes as parameters.
